Aadhaar Card Verifier

How It Works?

Welcome to the Aadhaar Card Verifier, an easy-to-use tool that allows you to check the validity of your Aadhaar Card and extract important information such as your Name, Date of Birth (DOB), and Aadhaar Number. Simply upload a PDF, JPG, or PNG file of your Aadhaar card, click the "Verify" button, and within a few seconds, you will receive the results.

Key Features:

  • Aadhaar Number Validation using the Verhoeff algorithm.
  • Information Extraction: Name, DOB, and Aadhaar Number.
  • File Recommendations: Upload the highest quality document possible for best results.
